Technical SEO

Get found on Google. Built into the code — not bolted on after.

See what's included +

Technical foundation

Title tag & meta descriptionHand-written, keyword-targeted copy that controls how your site appears in Google — the first impression before anyone clicks.
Canonical URLPrevents duplicate content penalties when your site lives on multiple domains (Vercel + custom domain).
XML sitemap + robots.txtA complete map of your site submitted to Google so every page gets crawled and indexed fast.
Geo meta tagsTies your site to a specific city and coordinates — a local signal that helps you rank for "[service] near me" searches.

On-page & keyword strategy

Keyword research & placementResearch-backed keywords woven into headlines, body copy, and image alt text — so Google knows exactly what you do and where you do it.
Image alt textEvery image tagged with descriptive, keyword-rich alt text — improving both search visibility and accessibility.
Open Graph + Twitter CardControls how your site previews when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, iMessage, and X — branded thumbnail included.

Structured data (schema)

LocalBusiness / MedicalBusinessTells Google your name, address, hours, and service area so you can appear in the map pack and local rich results.
Physician / Person schemaFor practices and personal brands — surfaces credentials, specialties, and affiliations directly in search.
FAQPage schemaMakes your FAQ answers appear as expandable rich snippets in Google — more visibility, more clicks, no extra ad spend.
Service / Procedure schemaMarks up your core offerings so Google can match you to exactly the right high-intent searches.
